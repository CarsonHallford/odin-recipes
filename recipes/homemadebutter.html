Homemade Butter

Homemade butter is a fun and delicious way to make a versatile and beloved ingreident to keep around the house.

Ingredients

  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Stand Mixer

Steps

  1. Add heavy whipping cream to a stand mixer.
  2. Set stand mixer to a speed of 2 or 3 and let mix until thick peaks are developed.
  3. Stop the mixer and scrape down the sides of the bowl.
  4. Continue mixing until butter begins to form and separates from the buttermilk
  5. Take butter out of the bowl and into another bowl of ice cold water.
  6. Wash the butter to remove any excess buttermilk. Repeat this step until water runs clear.
  7. Form butter to your liking and wrap in parchment paper or plastic wrap and set in fridge.
