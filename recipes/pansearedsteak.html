Pan Seared Steak

A pan seared steak is a great choice for breakfast, lunch or dinner. It is easy to prepare, and enjoyed by many. Its ingredient list is short, alongside its preparation.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of steak: New York Strip, Ribeye, Filet Mignon, etc
  • High quality butter.
  • Garlic cloves
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh thyme and rosemary
  • High-smoke point cooking oil

Steps

  1. Pat your steak dry with paper towels and season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Pre-heat your choice of skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Once pre-heated, add a tablespoon of high-smoke point cookingoil.
  4. Lay the steak facing away and leave undisturbed for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Flip steak and add the remaining ingredients.
  6. Use a cooking spoon and baste the steak with the melted butter and aromatics.
  7. Allow steak to rest covered for half the cooking time.
  8. Slice against the grain and enjoy.
